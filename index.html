Our Streamlined & Secure ITAD Process

Transparent Steps for Compliant IT Asset Disposition, From Start to Finish

1

Initial Consultation & Detailed Quote

Request a quote and receive a detailed proposal within 24 hours covering IT asset recovery potential, secure disposal, and certified data destruction services.

2

Scope of Work Agreement

Review and approve a formal Scope of Work (SOW) outlining specific ITAD services for your project or establish a Master Service Agreement (MSA) for recurring needs.

3

Secure Logistics & On-Site Collection

Schedule secure, white-glove IT asset removal managed by our vetted logistics team. We maintain strict chain-of-custody protocols from your location to our processing facility.

4

Secure Processing & Data Sanitization

IT assets are inventoried, tested, and graded. Data-bearing devices undergo certified data sanitization compliant with NIST 800-88 standards, then prepared for value recovery or recycling.

5

Value Recovery & Responsible Recycling

Eligible assets are refurbished and remarketed for maximum value recovery. Non-reusable equipment is responsibly recycled through R2v3 certified processes.

6

Audit-Ready Reporting & Reconciliation

Receive comprehensive, serialized reporting detailing final asset disposition, including Certificates of Destruction (CoD) and Certificates of Recycling (CoR) for compliance records.

Office Move IT FAQs

How far in advance should we schedule pickup?

Ideally 2-4 weeks before your move date, but we can accommodate shorter timelines. Contact us as soon as you know your move date for best scheduling flexibility.

Can you pick up on the same day as our movers?

Yes, we can coordinate day-of pickups. We recommend scheduling us for the morning with your movers arriving later, giving time to remove disposed equipment first.

What about equipment at the new location that needs disposal?

If you're moving into a space with existing IT equipment, we can include that in the same pickup. Just let us know during the quote process.

How quickly do we get certificates of destruction?

Standard delivery is within 30 days. Expedited options available if you need documentation sooner for compliance or audit purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Essential information for businesses considering professional ITAD services.

Do you work with small businesses? +
Absolutely! We work with businesses of all sizes - from startups with a handful of computers to Fortune 500 companies with thousands of assets. You get the same R2v3 certified processes, free data destruction at our facility, and professional certificates regardless of volume. No minimum requirements.
What certifications does ECOS maintain? +
ECOS Technologies maintains R2v3 certification (the highest ITAD standard), along with ISO 14001 (Environmental Management), ISO 9001 (Quality Management), and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety). We're among the top 6% of ITAD providers holding both R2v3 and triple ISO certification.
How is data destruction verified? +
Each drive receives a unique certificate of destruction including serial numbers, destruction method (NIST 800-88 compliant), date/time stamps, and technician certification. Video verification is available for high-security requirements. All certificates are delivered within 30 days via secure portal.
What's included in service agreements? +
Service agreements are available for businesses of all sizes. Small businesses get simplified contracts with flexible terms. Larger organizations receive dedicated account management, custom SLAs, scheduled pickups, preferential pricing, quarterly business reviews, and portal access. We scale our service to match your needs.
How does the revenue share program work? +
Equipment with positive residual value generates revenue share based on current market rates. After data destruction, assets are remarketed through certified channels. Clients receive detailed settlement reports showing asset values, processing costs, and net proceeds. Payment typically within 30-45 days.
What insurance coverage do you maintain? +
ECOS carries comprehensive insurance including General Liability, Professional Liability (E&O), Cyber Liability with data breach coverage, Pollution Liability, and Commercial Auto. Certificates of insurance are available upon request for all clients.
Do you handle data center decommissioning? +
Yes, we specialize in complete data center decommissioning including asset removal, de-installation, cable management, and facility restoration. Our team handles projects from single racks to entire facilities with minimal business disruption. Project management and detailed planning included.
What's your geographic coverage? +
ECOS provides nationwide service across all 50 states with direct operations - no subcontractors. Our own fleet and certified personnel ensure consistent service quality and security. Local pickup typically within 24-48 hours, nationwide logistics within 3-5 business days.
How do you handle high-security environments? +
We accommodate cleared facilities, secure areas, and environments requiring background checks. Our team includes personnel with security clearances. We offer witnessed destruction, on-site services, and can work within your security protocols including escorts and restricted access areas.

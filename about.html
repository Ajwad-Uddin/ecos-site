About ECOS Technologies

Based in Tucker, Georgia, ECOS Technologies provides comprehensive IT asset disposition services to businesses nationwide. With over 22 years of industry leadership experience, we've built a reputation for reliability, compliance, and value.

We understand that every business has unique ITAD needs. That's why we offer flexible solutions - from single computer pickups to complete data center decommissioning. Our hybrid approach allows us to scale services to match your requirements perfectly.

As one of the few ITAD providers holding both R2v3 certification and ISO 14001, 9001, and 45001 standards, we ensure your assets are handled with the highest levels of security, quality, and environmental responsibility.

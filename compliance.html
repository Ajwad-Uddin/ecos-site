R2v3 Certification

Responsible Recycling Standard - Version 3

The R2v3 certification is the most recent and comprehensive standard for responsible recycling practices in the electronics industry. This certification validates our commitment to:

  • Proper handling of focus materials and hazardous substances
  • Complete data destruction with verified processes
  • Downstream vendor accountability and auditing
  • Environmental, health, and safety management systems
  • Legal compliance with all federal and state regulations
